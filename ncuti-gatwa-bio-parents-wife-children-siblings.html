Ncuti Gatwa Bio, Parents, Wife, Children, Siblings, Net Worth

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, March 16, 2024

Ncuti Gatwa Bio, Parents, Wife, Children, Siblings, Net Worth: Born on October 15, 1992, Ncuti Gatwa, officially known as Mizero Ncuti Gatwa is a Rwandan-Scottish actor.

He developed a passion for acting at a younger age and eventually began his acting journey on stage at the Dundee Repertory Theatre.

He has been consistent throughout his career and has risen to become one of the most sought-after actors.

Gatwa is best known for his role as Eric Effiong (a gay teenager) on the Netflix comedy-drama series Sex Education (2019–present).

His Eric Effiong role earned him a BAFTA Scotland Award for Best Actor in Television and three BAFTA Television Award nominations for Best Male Comedy Performance.

He was nominated for an Ian Charleson Award for his performance as Mercutio in a 2014 production of Romeo & Juliet at HOME.

In 2015, Gatwa appeared in a supporting role in the miniseries Stonemouth, an adaptation of the 2012 novel of the same name.

He also played Demetrius in the 2016 production of A Midsummer Night’s Dream at Shakespeare’s Globe directed by Emma Rice.

In April 2022 Gatwa was cast in Greta Gerwig’s Barbie and that same year (2002), he was cast as the fifteenth incarnation of the lead role in Doctor Who.

In July 2023, Ncuti Gatwa hit the headlines after an official Doctor Who image (a new promotional shot) was shared.

The new promotional shot provides fans with a special look at Ncuti Gatwa as the 15th Doctor ahead of Season 14.

Gatwa is then expected to take over as the 15th Doctor from December’s Christmas special, which will lead into Season 14.

Set to release in 2024, Doctor Who season 14 will comprise 8 episodes and will be available to stream in the United States on Disney+.

Ncuti Gatwa Age

Ncuti Gatwa celebrated his 30th birthday in October last year (2022). He was born on October 15, 1992, in Nyarugenge, Kigali, Rwanda. Gatwa will be 31 in October this year (2023).

Ncuti Gatwa Education

Ncuti Gatwa attended Boroughmuir High School and Dunfermline High School.

Later he studied at the Royal Conservatoire of Scotland, graduating with a Bachelor of Arts in Acting in 2013.

The Conservatoire awarded him an honorary Doctorate at the class of 2022 graduation ceremony

Ncuti Gatwa Parents

Ncuti Gatwa was born in Nyarugenge, Kigali, Rwanda to his parents. Though he’s famous, there’s no information about the actor’s mother.

However, his father is known as Dr. Tharcisse Gatwa, a journalist with a Ph.D. in theology who hails from Rwanda’s Karongi District.

The family escaped from Rwanda during the Rwandan genocide against the Tutsi in 1994 and settled in Scotland.

Erica Mena Accuses Bow Wow of Beating Women In Twitter Battle: Troubled Couple Split For Good

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, August 27, 2024

Erica Mena went there on Twitter after a below the belt comment from Bow Wow enraged the reality star and after Mena accused her former fiance of being a woman beater, there’s no way he’ll take her back again.

The Twitter battle began on Wednesday, December 10 when Erica tweeted, “Who wants to speed date when motherf****** ain’t worth sh*t?” Apparently that didn’t sit well with Mena’s rapper turned actor ex because Bow Wow responded on his own account by sharing pictures of himself and his baby mama Joie Chavis inside the house he formerly shared with Mena.

The first of Bow Wow’s upsetting pictures was captioned, “Got my daughter mom going up on the gram.” There was no explanation for the photo or even news that the CSI: Cyber actor was back together with his former ex.

Then the rapper, who would rather be known as his real name Shad Moss, captioned the second photo of himself and Joie Chavis with, “We all deserve to be happy.”

That’s when the social media battle we’ve all been waiting for began. Everyone knows that Erica Mena is a fighter and she’ll stoop as low as it takes to win. So Mena threw down the gloves and blasted Bow Wow with accusations that he was abusive to Chavis.

Apparently Erica moved out of Shad’s house a little over a month ago but never really had it out publically (which is very unusual for Mena.) The former BET star and his reality star girlfriend threw enough hints on social media that many assumed they were working on their relationship and a reunion announcement was expected at any time.

If there was any doubt that Erica Mena and Bow Wow are over for good, there isn’t any now. Mena spoke to Global Grind after the blowup online. Mena didn’t mince words and we hope she’s being honest because the allegations she waged against the CSI: Cyber actor are pretty serious.

Mena told Global Grind, “I’m not going to sit there and let my child see me be mistreated by a man.” Mena claims that Shad Moss trying to mistreat her and that he had been abusive with his baby mama Chavis, Erica went on to say, “I’ve been quiet about it because we were engaged, but obviously he was in his feelings after my Snapchat post.”

Mena claimed that Bow Wow was really trying to get under her skin by letting the reality star know that he was hanging out with Joie Chavis again. Of course Mena claims it didn’t work but then she unleashed a whole lot of fury. We’re guessing Mena was really mad and that’s why she spilled the dirty details that she says Shad Moss is trying to keep quiet.

“I never wanted (his) business out there. I feel like people have seen me on reality TV having to fight, but at this point in my life, I deserve peace. I’m in a different place now. When it comes to Shad, this is the man I at one point really considered marrying. He is the one I decided to say yes to, but once the mask came off, I realized I had made a bad choice,” Mena confessed.

Then Erica Mena dropped an even bigger bomb on us. The former Love & Hip Hop: New York troublemaker confirmed that she is no longer on the show. It’s looking like Erica has no plans to be on another reality show either. Mena has made it pretty clear that right now all she wants is for Bow Wow to leave her alone and let her just have some peace and quiet.

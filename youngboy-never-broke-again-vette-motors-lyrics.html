About

YoungBoy Never Broke Again, also known as NBA YoungBoy or simply YoungBoy, is an American rapper, singer, and songwriter. He was born on October 20, 1999, in Baton Rouge, Louisiana.

YoungBoy started his music career in 2015 with the release of his debut mixtape “Life Before Fame”. However, he gained widespread attention in 2017 with his hit single “Outside Today,” which peaked at No. 31 on the Billboard Hot 100 chart.

Since then, YoungBoy has released a number of successful projects, including mixtapes such as “38 Baby” and “AI YoungBoy 2,” as well as studio albums like “Top” and “Sincerely, Kentrell.” His music often touches on subjects such as street life, relationships, and struggles with mental health.

Despite his young age, YoungBoy has already had a significant impact on the hip-hop industry. He has collaborated with several notable artists, including DaBaby and Juice WRLD, and has been praised for his unique style and energetic performances. However, he has also faced legal issues throughout his career, including arrests for weapons charges and alleged involvement in violent crimes.

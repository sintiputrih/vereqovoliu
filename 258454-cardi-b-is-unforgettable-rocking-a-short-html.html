Cardi B Is Unforgettable Rocking a Short Haircut with a Blazer and Trendy Tie See Her Look

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, July 26, 2024

Cardi B, famous for her impressive rap talent and ability to whip up eye-catching looks, stuns fans with an unforgettable appearance in a short haircut, a long tail blazer, and trendy tie.

Cardi B made her mark in the entertainment industry within a few years of her emergence. She has won musical awards and amassed millions of fans online and offline.

The "Bodak Yellow" crooner's extraordinary rap tone and lyrics are not the only things that set her apart from her colleagues. The rapper has zero tolerance for the naysayers and delights in clamping down on them when she deems fit.

Cardi B at the Fashion Nova x Cardi B Collection Launch Party on May 08, 2019 in Los Angeles, California | Photo: Getty Images

That is not all, the celebrity is ever ready to make fashion statements and shows that she feels comfortable in her unique style in fashionable closet selections.

Recently, she dazzled millions of followers wearing a black ensemble. Cardi B dressed in a unique blazer with a neck cut that displayed a chunk of cleavage.

She added a black tie that hung down her chest and opted for fishnet and ankle-high boots. The trendy look came with an ecstatic pose showing part of her glazing makeup and black hair. The photo's caption read:

It was not certain whether she modeled the outfit for a brand, but many fans found it inspiring as they praised the look and confessed that it was something worth emulating.

The 28-year-old also earns the admiration of celebrities in the entertainment industry. A few weeks ago, she served fans a chic and sassy look.

This time, there was no display of too much skin, except an inch of her bare belly that could be seen between the designer crop top and mini skirt rocked by the rapper.

She paired it with white heels and a blue bag while being sun-kissed in front of a set of tall and wide-growing green plants. This attracted comments from rapper Remy Ma and celebrities like Chloe Bailey and Donna Marie Lombardi.

Making good music, looking good, and putting trolls in their lanes is not the only thing on Cardi B's list. During an interview, she revealed that she is proud of her awards and achievements but would want to make a lasting legacy through her assets.

Cardi B, who shares a two-year-old daughter with her husband Offset, is also open to sharing family secrets. She explained that the father of her child and lover gives her essential advice for contracts and is great with numbers.

This is something cherished by the entertainer, but she has also confessed to learning the ropes of smart business deals from a young age!

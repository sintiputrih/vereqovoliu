The Challenge Analyse Talavera Boyfriend Jack Matthews

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, September 7, 2024

A social media marketeer and brand influencer, Analyse Talavera, is most famously known for participating in the CBS show Big Brother in 2019, where she became close with her partner Jack Matthews.

Talavera is a rookie on The Challenge: Ride or Dies season, seeking to outperform the other competitors.

After the debut special, the show’s new season will begin on October 12 at 8 pm ET. The TJ Lavin-hosted program’s cast, which consists of both seasoned fans and fresh faces, will compete each week in a mental and physical competition for a portion of $1 million.

The Challenge: Analyse Talavera Boyfriend, Jack Matthews, And Their Age Difference

The three then embarked on a cross-country road trip to the West Coast, stopping in Louisiana, Nevada, and Colorado. Matthews then visited Michie and a few of their friends in Dallas, Texas, for a “boy’s trip.”

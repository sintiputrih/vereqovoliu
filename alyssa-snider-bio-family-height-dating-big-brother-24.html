Meet Alyssa Snider, Big Brother 24 contestant. She hailed from Sarasota, Florida.

Two episodes in, dramas have ensued already. Two beautiful women Taylor Hale and Paloma Aguilar are competing against each other, at least Paloma is. Taylor was bashed so much by her housemates that she broke down at one point. But BB Alums from outside of the house have shown their support to her.

While she competes in the show, let’s learn a few things about her that you don’t see about her in the show. This article covers everything from age, job, family members, and height.

So, keep on scrolling to learn more about Alyssa Snider.

Alyssa Snider On Big Brother 24

Alyssa Snider is one of the 16 houseguests on Big Brother season 24. There is a range of cast members from various professional backgrounds and age groups.

Everyone has eyes on the prize so she is going in as herself hoping for the best. Alyssa thinks people will perceive her as a young and go-with-the-flow bubbly girl and likely mistake for underestimating her. Speaking with Parade, she added that she looks forward to working with Loud, obnoxious players.

But, Alyssa thinks that her talkative nature is something her fellow housemates might find annoying. As for the eviction night, she is likely to go with the house vote. She said, “From what I’ve seen in Big Brother when you flip the vote, they always find out who did it, and then you’re next to go”.

Other trivia about her includes her love for writing poetry. Her family played games similar to the show. She also knows Alyssa Lopez from season 23 as they share the same hometown. Moreover, they also worked in the same industry since Lopez is a swimwear designer and Snider revealed in the first episode that she’s a customer service rep for a swimwear brand.

Last summer, Sarasota swimwear designer Alyssa competed on “Big Brother” and survived 65 days before she was ousted by a powerful six-person alliance. Lopez is competing once again, this time on the series “The Challenge USA,” a network version of a long-running MTV series.

This year’s edition features 28 veterans of other CBS reality series, including “Survivor,” “The Amazing Race” and “Love Island.” Lopez is one of six houseguests from season 23 of “Big Brother” on the show, which is hosted by BMX star TJ Lavin.

Alyssa is hoping to win $750,000 as the last man standing in late September. But there is a variety of competitions, including weekly Head of Household challenges, and form alliances to try to avoid being eliminated in weekly votes. Some win rewards each week while others face penalties, like eating only “slop”.

Is Alyssa Snider Dating Anyone?

At the time when she entered Big Brother, Alyssa Snider was single. But, she has shared a picture of her prom date on her IG. She captioned, “we didn’t come to play”. The man’s name is Daniel Plattner who earned B.S. in Physiology and Kinesiology.

Alyssa Snider Family

Alyssa Snider is the daughter of Chris Snider and Traci Snider. In May 2020, she wished Chris a birthday via an IG post. She wrote, “Happy birthday to this hilarious, overprotective, loving father of mine. My best friend from the jump”.

In 2022, Chris turned 58 years old. According to Chris’s Facebook, he is happily retired since 2019. Meanwhile, her mother Traci is 56 years old being born in August 1965.

Alyssa also has a brother named Kyle Snider. Born in May 1994, he is 28 years old. He is currently working as a Crypto Currency Asset Manager at CAM inc. since March 2018. He also worked as an interactive training coordinator at CRM Suite. Coming to his academic qualification, he attended Sarasota County Techincal Institute where he studied business management.

Moreover, he also attended Manatee Technical College.

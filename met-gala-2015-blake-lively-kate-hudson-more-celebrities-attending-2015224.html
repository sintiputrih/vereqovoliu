Met Gala 2015: Find Out Which Celebs Are Attending Fashion's Big Bash!

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, August 29, 2024

Fashion's biggest night is almost here!

There are less than two weeks to go until the Costume Institute Gala 2015, which will take place at NYC's famed Metropolitan Museum of Art on Monday, May 4. And while the theme ("China: Through the Looking Glass") and celebrity co-chair (Jennifer Lawrence) were announced well in advance, one question still remains: Who's going to the ball?

A source confirmed to Us Weekly that Kerry Washington, Jennifer Lopez, and Zoe Kravitz are all set to attend the Met Gala. But Fashionista managed to get its digital paws on a nearly complete list of the celebs, models, and designers who will be hitting up the event, and it includes Blake Lively, Reese Witherspoon, and Kim Kardashian, too!

Scroll down to see who else will be strolling up the steps of the Met.

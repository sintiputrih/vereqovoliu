Career

Haynes started his modeling career when he was fifteen years old in New York City, New York. Following that, he was featured in a Bruce Weber photo session for Abercrombie & Fitch. Haynes then started modeling for Kira Plastinina, J. C. Penney, and Ralph Lauren. Colton Haynes Wife & Marriage

Colton Haynes has battled anxiety his whole life. After years of public conjecture about his “hidden homosexual background,” an off-the-cuff reaction he made on Tumblr about a comment on images published in gay lifestyle magazine XY a decade ago was interpreted as evidence of his coming out by numerous media sources in January 2016. On March 11, 2017, Haynes and renowned florist Jeff Leatham got engaged. On October 27, 2017, they married. Haynes filed for divorce from Leatham in May 2018, but the couple reunited before their first wedding anniversary. They did, however, finalize a divorce settlement in August 2019.

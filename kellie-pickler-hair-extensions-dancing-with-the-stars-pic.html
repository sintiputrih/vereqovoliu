Kellie Picklers Hair Extensions DWTS Stars Crazy New Look Hollywood Life

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, July 28, 2024

Kellie’s got a brand new ‘do! The up and coming ballroom dancer tweeted her fans on April 23 showing off her new big, blonde hairdo. Do you like these extra curly extensions on Kellie?

Country music star Kellie Pickler is spicing up her look again as she’s decided to go back to wearing extensions for Dancing With The Stars. The starlet took to Twitter to post a photo of her big and bouncy hairdo right before wowing the judges with her dance routine on April 23. “Wiggin’ out again on #DWTS #RockinLocks :-),” tweeted the Kellie. Her blonde, curly hair extensions complimented her performance attire while defining the outline of her flawless face perfectly. Do you like this big and beautiful hairdo better than her buzz cut?

Kellie Pickler Cuts Hair Super Short To Support Friend With Cancer

Kellie has been wearing wigs and extensions ever since she made the bold and beautiful move to chop off her gorgeous locks in September 2012. The singer’s drastic decision was in honor of her best friend, Summer Holt Miller, who was diagnosed with breast cancer and is undergoing treatment.

“I cannot thank everyone enough for all the love and support you’ve given Summer,” Kellie tweeted after the extreme, short haircut. “Chemo starts tomorrow. #SayAPrayerForSummer”

Kellie’s brave action is just one part of this inspirational journey she is on to help her long-time friend win her battle against cancer. Since the major chop, the former American idol star has received nothing but love and support from family, friends, and even some Hollywood stars.

The extensions definitely match her dramatic ballroom costumes!

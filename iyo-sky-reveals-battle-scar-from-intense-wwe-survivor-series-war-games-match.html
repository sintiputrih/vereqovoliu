Iyo Sky Reveals Battle Scar from Intense WWE Survivor Series War Games Match

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, July 7, 2024

Survivor Series 2023 delivered an unforgettable Women’s War Games match that saw Becky Lynch, Charlotte Flair, Shotzi, and Bianca Belair triumph over Damage CTRL’s formidable team of Bayley, Asuka, Kairi Sane, and Iyo Sky. The intense physicality of the bout left its mark on the competitors, quite literally.

In the aftermath of the grueling match, WWE Women’s Champion Iyo Sky took to social media to proudly display a “battle scar” she received during the Women’s War Games. SKY, who delivered a memorable dive off the top of the cage with a trash can over her head, was undeniably one of the standout performers of the match.

With Bayley absorbing the loss for Damage CTRL, the fallout from the Women’s War Games promises intrigue as fans await to see how the faction’s other members will respond to her failure.

Ringside News will continue to provide updates and insights into the aftermath of WWE Survivor Series 2023 as more information becomes available.

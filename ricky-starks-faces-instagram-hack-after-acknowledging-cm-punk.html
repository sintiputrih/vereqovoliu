Ricky Starks Faces Instagram Hack After Acknowledging CM Punk

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, June 28, 2024

Ricky Starks is currently one of the highlights of AEW television, and there is a good reason for that. Starks has worked hard to establish himself as a solid talent, and fans continue to support him all the time. He recently gave CM Punk his flowers in a post and now it appears Starks claimed that his account was hacked.

CM Punk was the star of AEW Collision after its debut back in June and Ricky Starks was one of his main opponents. They feuded with each other and wrestled on numerous occasions. After his firing, Starks gave CM Punk his flowers.

However, Ricky Starks took to his Instagram stories and claimed that his account was hacked, where he noted his appreciation to CM Punk was the result of a hack. In the images he uploaded, Starks clarified that he got it all cleared up and thanked fans for letting him know something was fishy with his account. He also hoped that no one ended up clicking on the link in his stories.

Ok I got it all cleared up. Thanks for letting me know something was fishy about the acct lol.

I hope no one clicked the link. Account was hacked/spam but we back.

Ricky Starks will always have respect for CM Punk no matter what, but it appears that post might not have been by him in the end. Regardless, we’ll have to wait and see what’s next in store for Ricky Starks.

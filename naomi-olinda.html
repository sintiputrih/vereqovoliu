Naomie Olindo wiki, bio, net worth, nose job, lawsuit, boyfriend, marriage, kids.

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, April 20, 2024

Naomie Olindo is a French-born American businesswoman, reality television star, and social media personality. She is best known for appearing in the Bravo TV show Southern Charm, where she showcases her life as a young entrepreneur and socialite in Charleston, South Carolina. Naomie is also the co-founder of L’Abeye, a lifestyle brand that offers clothing, accessories, and home goods.

Early Life and Education

Naomie was born on August 4, 1992, in Nice, France. She is the daughter of Joel and Carole Olindo, who are both French natives. She has a younger sister whose identity is not known. Naomie moved to the United States when she was 13 years old and settled in Charleston, South Carolina. She attended the College of Charleston, where she majored in finance and graduated in 2014. She then pursued a master’s degree in business administration at the same college.

Professional Career:

Naomie started her career as a model and actress, appearing in commercials and short films. She also worked as an account executive at a marketing agency called Levelwing.

Southern Charm:

In 2016, she made her debut on the reality television show Southern Charm, as the girlfriend of Craig Conover, one of the main cast members. The show follows the personal and professional lives of several socialites in Charleston, who belong to prominent families or have ties to the city’s history and culture. Naomie became a fan favorite for her outspokenness, honesty, and style. She also shared her struggles with anxiety and body image issues on the show.

She dated Craig for three years, but they broke up in 2017 due to their differences and lack of compatibility. She then started dating Metul Shah, an anesthesiologist, in 2018. They had a long-distance relationship for a while, as Metul moved to New York City for his fellowship. Naomie left the show in 2020 and moved to New York City with Metul. She said that she wanted to focus on her career and well-being, and that she had outgrown the drama of the show. However, they split up in 2021 after Naomie discovered that Metul had cheated on her.

Naomie returned to the show in 2021 as a guest for season 8. She revealed that she had hooked up with her ex-boyfriend Craig after breaking up with Metul, but that it was nothing more than a casual fling.

Business and Lawsuits:

In 2018, Naomie co-founded L’Abeye with her friend Ginny Cox. L’Abeye is a lifestyle brand that offers clothing, accessories, and home goods that are inspired by Naomie’s French heritage and Southern charm. The brand aims to empower women to feel confident and beautiful in their own skin. However, the partnership comes to a bitter end in May 2022 when Cox is terminated from L’Abeye for employee theft and other misconduct, according to Olindo. Cox came with a lawsuit on September 19, 2022: Cox files a lawsuit against Olindo for breach of contract, fraud, defamation and more, claiming that Olindo used deceptive tactics to maintain control of the business, misappropriate funds and prevent her from landing a new job. Olindo responds to the lawsuit with her own counterclaims, accusing Cox of theft, extortion and defamation. Olindo alleges that Cox hacked into her computer to access her American Express account and used points on the credit card to vacation with her boyfriend in Florida. Olindo also claims that Cox stole clothing from the apparel company and sold them at local consignment shops for her own personal profit. The lawsuit is still ongoing as of May 2023.

Personal Life

Boyfriend, Marriage, Kids:

Naomie Olindo is neither married nor has any kids but she indeed is in a relationship with Frank Kenan, a member of the illustrious Kenan family.  Before dating Kenan, Olindo was in a relationship with Dr. Metul Shah, an anesthesiologist, for three years. They broke up in July 2021 after allegations of Shah’s cheating surfaced on social media. Olindo and Shah had moved to New York City together from Charleston shortly before their split.

Olindo has also dated Craig Conover, another Southern Charm star, for three years. They broke up in 2017 and remained friends afterward. 

A loss much closer to home:

Naomi has seen her fair share of heartbreaks and the socialite mourned the death of her father. Joel passed on 20th December 2019 due to esophageal cancer. After the tragic loss of her father, Naomi took to Instagram to share the loss. Her post read: “I lost a piece of my heart last Friday. After privately fighting esophageal cancer for 12 excruciating months, my dad lost the only fight he’s ever lost. He died peacefully surrounded by people that loved him more than words can explain. I missed him before he was even gone, and I can’t imagine what this road will be like.”

A nose job?

Naomi has never tried to hide that she went under the knife. The reality star claims that she went through it for herself and not for anyone else. From answering fans’ questions regarding her nose job to advocating for plastic surgery, it seems Naomi is pretty happy with the result.

  • Naomie had a nose job in 2018 and documented her recovery on Instagram. She said that she had wanted to get her nose done since she was eight years old and that she had no pain or regrets.
  • Naomie’s plastic surgeon was Dr. Joshua Zimm, an NYC-based facial plastic and reconstructive surgeon. He performed a rhinoplasty surgery on Naomie, which is a procedure that reshapes the nose to improve its appearance and function.
  • Naomie shared before and after photos of her nose job on Instagram, showing the difference in her profile and three-quarter angle. She said that she was very happy with the results and that her surgeon did an amazing job.
  • Naomie also defended herself against critics who slammed her for getting the surgery. She said that plastic surgery shouldn’t be taboo and that she did it for herself and no one else.

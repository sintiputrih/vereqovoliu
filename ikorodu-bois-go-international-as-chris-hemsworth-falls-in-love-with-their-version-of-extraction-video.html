Nigerian Instagram comedy group Ikorodu Bois has gone international as American actor Chris Hemsworth reposts their version of ‘Extraction’ trailer.

The group is known to be very good at mimicking celebrities and also remaking popular movies as they went viral with their version of the ‘Money Heist’ trailer.

Earlier today, the producers of the Nexflix movie ‘Extraction’ reacted to the remake of the trailer by these young ones promising them to be at the premiere of season 2.

Chris Hemsworth while reacting to the remake of the movie trailer fell in love with the good work of the young boys as he says their version might be better than the original.

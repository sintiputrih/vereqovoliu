Kylies Love Bite Lip Kit Looks Like an Actual Hickey: Pic

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, January 20, 2024

Sink your teeth into this. Kylie Jenner launched three new Lip Kits in August 2016, including one with a very sexy name: Love Bite. She shared the shade in a series of August 25 Snapchat stories. Along with Dirty Peach and Brown Sugar, fans snatched up the deep mauve matte liquid lipstick and shared their own selfies and swatches.

@kyliecosmetics @KylieJenner new swatch is true to its name. hope you enjoy. RT pic.twitter.com/Wd2BVqZGCr

— kashley kardashian (@kashleykardash) September 9, 2016

But one tweeter, Ashley Elizabeth, took things a step further on Friday, September 9. Instead of pouting her painted lips for the camera, she compared Love Bite to, well, an actual love bite. “@kyliecosmetics @KylieJenner new swatch is true to its name. hope you enjoy. RT,” she wrote in a post. In the photo, Ashley presumably held up a tube of the matte liquid lipstick along with the box to a hickey on the side of someone else’s neck. (“so it’s not my hickey guys,” Ashley later tweeted.) 

She racked up at least 8,000 shares and 20,000 favorites, but it made Ashley’s “entire day” when Jenner, 19, retweeted the funny post. 

“YO KYLIE JENNER RETWEETED ME OMG IM DEAD YO OMG,” she wrote in all caps. “IM CRYING.”

The Kylie Lip Kit entrepreneur may have noticed that tweet, but no word on another one that Ashley’s boyfriend, Mike, sent out later that day.

“@KylieJenner My girlfriend @Ashsardella8 got you some free advertisement today, and her iPhone broke… So, #HelpHerOut #LoveBite,” @MykulMitch posted.

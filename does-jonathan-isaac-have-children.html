Does Jonathan Isaac have children? Jonathan Isaac Biography

Jonathan Isaac was born on 3rd October 1997 in Naples, Florida. His mother is known as Jackie Allen. There is currently no information about who his father is. The American basketball player also has one older sister, two older brothers, and two younger brothers. He attended Florida State University where he played college basketball for the Seminoles.

In 2017, Jonathan Isaac was drafted by the Orlando Magic as the sixth overall pick in the 2017 NBA Draft. Isaac went made his NBA debut from the 2017 to 2018 season. He played in 27 games and averaged 5.4 points and 3.7 rebounds per game. Jonathan continued to improve his performance in the 2018 to 2019 season. Isaac then became a regular starter for the Magic and averaged 9.6 points and 5.5 rebounds per game. In the 2019 to 2020 season, he was named to the NBA All-Defensive Second Team and played in the Rising Stars Challenge during the All-Star Weekend.

Isaac has been missing due to injury however, on 10 January 2023, he was assigned one game for the Magic G League affiliate. On January 23 2023, he returned again after 2 and half years of absence. He recorded 10 points, 3 rebounds, and 2 steals in 9 minutes in a 113–98 win over the Boston Celtics.

Does Jonathan Isaac have children?

Jonathan Isaac does not have any children at the moment. The 25-year-old basketball player is engaged to Takita Nicole. They were reportedly engaged around 2020 and are planning to marry soon.

Can You Tell Which Larry David Is Older? (We Doubt It)

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, September 13, 2024

When the first season of Curb Your Enthusiasm aired in 2000, Larry David was 54 years old. For comparison, that is how old Paul Rudd is right now. But the benefit of looking older when you’re young is that it’s hard for others to tell the difference when you age. To the untrained eye, once a man is bald, he is sorted into the category of “old.” As a result, when fans picture Larry David on Curb, they see the same person during the final season as they did for the first, 24 years ago. To prove it, with the final episode of Curb Your Enthusiasm airing this weekend, we’ve updated our Larry David age quiz, which was originally published in 2017. For each question, you will be presented with two pretty, pretty good images of LD and you must determine which photo is the older LD. Good luck. You’ll need it.

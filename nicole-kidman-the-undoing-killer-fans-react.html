The Undoing: Who killed Elena in the thriller starring Nicole Kidman, Hugh Grant?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, June 21, 2024

The thrilling mini-series is available to watch on HBO in the US, and NOW TV and Sky Atlantic in the UK

    Nicole Kidman and Hugh Grant play husband and wife Grace and Jonathan Fraser in , which was released in the US over the weekend, and in the UK on Monday.

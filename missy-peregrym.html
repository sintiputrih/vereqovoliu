Missy Peregrym - Bio, Age, Net Worth, Married, Facts, Career

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, June 25, 2024

Table of Biography

Missy Peregrym is a well-known Canadian actress and ex-model. Missy Peregrym is famous for her role in the TV series FBI and also as the celebrity wife of Australian actor Tom Oakley.

Early Life

Missy Peregrym was born on 16 June 1982 and she is currently 41 years old. She hails from Montreal, Quebec, Canada, and her zodiac sign is Gemini. Likewise, her birth name is Melissa Peregrym. Talking about her family, her parents are her father Rev. Darrell Peregrym, and her mother Vanessa Peregrym. Her mother is a housewife whereas her father is a Pentecostal minister.

Career and Professional Life

Professionally, Missy Peregrym is a famous actress and was a fashion model in the past. People recognize her most for her roles as Haley Graham in Stick It and Officer Andy McNally in Rookie Blue. For the latter, this actress received the nomination for a Canadian Screen Award in the year 2016. Likewise, the actress has appeared in the CBS series FBI since the year 2018.

Besides her career as an actress, she is also well-known for her charity work. In the year 2006, the actress collaborated with the shoe company – TOMS. This company also donates safe footwear to communities in South America. The actress has also shared her experiences in Argentina and the situation of the communities she visited.

Talking more about the beginning of her career, the actress initially started as a professional model at the young age of 18 years. Her first agency was the Lizbell Agency. Her agency encouraged her to move over to commercials during her early modeling career. As a result, she modeled for commercials for Mercedes-Benz, Sprint Canada, and the Olympic Games shifting from print to screen.

Then in the year 2000, she made her debut as an actress. This was with an appearance in an episode of Dark Angel. Subsequently, the actress then had guest appearances on Heroes, The Chris Isaak Show, Fall of Heidi Fleiss, Black Sash, Life as We Know It, Jake 2.0, Smallville, Tru Calling, Andromeda, and Call Me: The Rise. Moreover, she also made a cameo appearance in Catwoman which is uncredited.

More Details

Following this, Missy made her film debut in the year 2006. It was with the lead role in Stick It. In the TV series Heroes, the actress appeared as Candice Wilmer. Likewise, the actress made her 1st lead appearance in a TV series in the year 2007. It was with the series Reaper and she played this role from 2007 to 2009.

Moving on, the actress had her big breakthrough in the year 2010. It was with the lead role in Rookie Blue. It is a Canadian TV series in which this actress played the role of police officer Andy McNally. After airing for six seasons, this series was canceled in the year 2015.

Then in the year 2012, the actress starred as Chloe Jocelyn in Cybergeddon. It is a web series by Yahoo.com. Then in the year 2013, the Canadian actress won a Streamy Award for her role in this web series. Then in the year 2014, she worked alongside Jeff Roop and Eric Balfour in the film Backcountry. Similarly, in the year 2016, the actress was nominated for a Canadian Screen Award. The nomination was for her role in Rookie Blue. Then in 2018, the actress began to appear as Agent Maggie Bell in the TV series FBI.

Relationship Status

Missy is a happily married woman. Her husband’s name is Tom Oakley. Tom is also an actor hailing from Australia. The lovers tied the knot on 30 December 2018 in Los Angeles. Together, the couple has a son named Otis Paradis Oakley. The lovers welcomed their child on 21 March 2020.

This marriage with Tom is her 2nd marriage. Before this relationship, the Canadian actress was married to the American actor, Zachary Levi. With Zachary, she tied the knot in June 2014. However, their marriage ended in a divorce after the actress filed for divorce in April 2015. Even before filing for divorce, the ex-couple had separated on 3 December 2014.

Stephen A Smith left in fits of laughter as JJ Redick reveals Molly Qerim's hidden item that didn't

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, May 2, 2024

FIRST Take guest analyst JJ Redick revealed Molly Qerim's multiple items hidden behind the studio desk. 

The former NBA star kicked things off on First Take unveiling Molly's water bottle sending Stephen A Smith into hysteria. 

The three were joined by NBA guest pundit and former player Tim Legler to discuss the NBA Draft. 

However, before things started, Stephen A called out Molly for having a glass on the desk.

"Just a technical thing that happens on television; usually, people don't have their drinks on the desk. They usually lower it."

Molly fired back that she would lower if she could.

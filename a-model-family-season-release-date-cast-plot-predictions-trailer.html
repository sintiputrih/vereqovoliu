A Model Family Season 2: Release Date, Cast, Plot, Trailer, And Everything You Need To Know!

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, June 21, 2024

Korean dramas are making a killing on Netflix and the newly released series adding to the collection is A Model Family. The series is less than a month old on Netflix, and so far, it has positive ratings and feedback from fans.

Directed by Kim Jin-Woo, the series takes the Ozark storytelling formula as it makes the main character the most desperate cast. It employs a unique plot and delivery, leaving you with nothing not to love about the series. With the first season out, the next question is how soon the second season will premiere on Netflix. Read some more to find out!

A Model Family Season 2 Release Date: When will it air?

The show’s first season was released on August 12th, 2022, and so far, it is unclear whether the first season will have a continuation. The showrunners of the show or the official channel are yet to announce when the show will premiere, and you will have to be a little patient. The series has a 6.6/10 on IMDB, and expecting a  second season is not too farfetched.

Considering that the show is less than a month old, the rating might go up in the coming days or months, pushing the series closer to its chance of renewal. In the case of a second season, the show will probably have ten episodes like the first season. 2023 is the most likely release year.

A Model Family Season 2 Cast: Who will be appearing?

The second season will consist of all the actors who survived and came out of season one alive. These actors include; Jung Woo as Dong-ha and Park Hee-soon starring as Gwang-Cheol. Yoon Jin-seo, Kim Sung-oh, Kim Shin-bi, Park Doo-shik, Shin Eun-soo, Heo Jin-na and Wong Hyun-jun. The second season might also include new casting.

A Model Family Season 2 Plot: What should we expect?

The show focuses on Park Dong-Ha, a hard-working guy who does not have enough to sustain himself. Intending to get a promotion at work, He uses money supposed to take his son for surgery, but the promotion backfires. Desperate to get more money, he has to bury some bodies and forever live with the consequences of his actions.

The first season ended with the Yongsoo Ring destroyed and Kwang –Chul in control of the drug trafficking business in South Korea. Joo-Hyun also murdered his mentor after realizing she had been working as a mole for the Yongsoo Ring. Dong-Ha also received an anonymous call threatening to harm his family if he surrendered to the police.

The second season could focus on the rivalry between the drug worlds. It could also focus on the threats uttered to Dong-ha and his relationship with Eun-Ju. All the mysteries will be answered in the second season.

A Model Family Season 2 Trailer: When will it be aired?

So far, there is no trailer for the second season of the crime show. The first season trailer was recently released, and the second season trailer might be out by 2023 if the show qualifies for a renewal.

In the mean time, there are a lot of Korean shows on Netflix that you can binge on as we await the official confirmation of the show’s renewal.

One of the biggest Korean games of all time is the “Squid Game.” Other popular Korean series on Netflix include Sweet home, Tomorrow and Hellbound.

Cara Delevingne Blasts Justin Bieber for Ranking Her Last as Hailey's Friends

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, July 22, 2024
  • Justin Bieber participated in the Spill Your Guts or Fill Your Guts segment on The Late Late Show with James Corden.
  • When asked to either eat a bull penis or rank wife Hailey Bieber's supermodel friends from "favorite to least favorite," Bieber chose to rank, subsequently placing Cara Delevingne last.
  • Delevingne responded in a now-deleted Instagram post.

When are men going to learn that sometimes it's better if you just shut up and eat a bull penis? Cara Delevingne tried to teach Justin Bieber this invaluable lesson after he participated in James Corden's Spill Your Guts or Fill Your Guts segment on The Late Late Show.

Faced with the option of either devouring the bull delicacy or ranking the supermodel friends of his wife, Hailey Bieber, from his "favorite to least favorite," Bieber made the mistake of ranking. In order, he placed Kendall Jenner as his favorite, followed by Hadid, then Delevingne.

He defended his choice, saying, "I know Kendall the best. I spent the most time with Kendall. She's a good friend of ours. I haven’t spent a lot of time with Gigi and I haven’t spent a lot of time with Cara, so I have nothing against those people."

You can watch the moment below starting around the 6:45 mark.

He might not have anything against Delevingne, but she definitely has something against him now. In an Instagram post that has since been deleted, the model responded, "If you have nothing against me, why don't you unblock me? Love you @haileybieber—he should have just eaten the bull penis."

To really bring the point home, she added a screenshot of Bieber on The Late Late Show and a picture of her walking down a runway while the singer gapes at her in the background. "Now vs. Then," she wrote.

Delevingne's girlfriend, Ashley Benson, supported the blast by promptly liking the post and commenting with laughing emojis, reports Marie Claire.

This isn't the first time Delevingne and Bieber have clashed. Last July, the singer wrote an apology to Taylor Swift for a "distasteful and insensitive" post he made in which he defended record executive Scooter Braun, whom Swift has publicly battled for the rights of her music. Wife Hailey lauded his apology, calling him a "gentleman."

Delevingne pushed back against Bieber's so-called apology, calling out the couple. “Gentleman? @haileybieber @justinbieber you must be bored,” she wrote in the comments. "I wish you spent less time sticking up for men and more time trying to understand women and respecting their valid reactions. As a married man, you should be lifting women up instead of tearing them down because you are threatened. I am not sure you actually understand what an apology is. This issue that @taylorswift is talking out is about far more than a picture and you know that. As you said, you haven’t spoken to her in years which means you definitely don’t understand the situation. I do. Take a step back and try to learn from this. We should all be on the same team. End of story.”

The ball (or should I say the bull?) is in your court now, Biebz.

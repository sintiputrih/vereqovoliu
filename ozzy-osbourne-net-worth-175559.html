Ozzy Osbourne is a legendary rock and roll performer who was born on December 3, 1948 in Aston, England. He is best known as the lead singer of Black Sabbath and has earned the nicknames of the Prince of Darkness and the Godfather of Heavy Metal. His influence on the rock and metal music genres is undeniable.

Before Fame

He was abused by teachers while growing up for having dyslexia and other learning disabilities, and worked as a plumber and a slaughterhouse employee while listening to The Beatles.

Family Life

He got married to Thelma Mayfair in 1971, and after their divorce in June 1982, he married Sharon Osbourne on July 4, 1982. He and Sharon split in 2016.

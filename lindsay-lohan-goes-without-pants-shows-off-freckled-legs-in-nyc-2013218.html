Lindsay Lohan Goes Without Pants Photo: Shows Off Freckled Legs

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, January 19, 2024

Forgetting something? Lindsay Lohan stepped out in New York City wearing a long white blouse and no pants on Tuesday, Aug. 20. The 27-year-old actress showed off her freckled legs in the daring ensemble while heading to dinner with hotelier Vikram Chatwal.

For her night out in the Big Apple, Lohan opted to wear nothing but a white dress shirt paired with gold ankle high-heeled boots, a colorful tote and a black choker necklace.

The Mean Girls star has been enjoying some newfound freedom after being released from a 90-day court-mandated rehab stay on July 30. In a sit-down interview with Oprah Winfrey on an episode of Oprah's Next Chapter which aired on Aug. 18, Lohan explained, "It's strange being confined for three months straight and then try to acclimate into real life."

What are inseam shorts?

The inseam of a pair of shorts is the length of the seam between the bottom of the crotch and the bottom of the shorts. Unlike pants which come in a wide range of fits like slim, skinny, relaxed, and so forth, shorts are primarily offered only by inseam length.

Why is chino called chino?

The first reference to chinos came in 1898, when American armed forces were stationed in the Philippines during the Spanish-American War and their uniforms were sourced from Chinese twill cotton. This colloquial term was born from the Spanish name for “China.”

What is a chino slang?

Chino (feminine china) was a casta term used in colonial Mexico to refer to people of mixed ancestry. In the eighteenth century, individuals of mixed Amerindian and African ancestry came to be called chinos.

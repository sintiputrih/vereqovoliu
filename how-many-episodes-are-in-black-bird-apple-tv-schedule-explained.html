HOW MANY EPISODES ARE IN BLACK BIRD? APPLE TV+ SCHEDULE EXPLAINED The Talks Today

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, August 21, 2024

Black Bird on Apple TV+ looks like it will be one of the best TV dramas of the year. It has an all-star cast and a plot that is based on real events.

Fans have been waiting for more episodes since the first two came out on the day the show started.

But how many episodes does Black Bird have? When does it come out? And what are the chances that there will be a second season?

BLACK BIRD RELEASE DATE AND PLOT PREVIEW

On Friday, July 8, 2022, Black Bird flew onto Apple TV

Based on real events, Black Bird is the story of James Keene, a former high school football star and the son of a decorated police officer who is sentenced to ten years in a minimum-security prison.

Keene is given the chance of a lifetime when he is offered his freedom in exchange for going to a maximum-security prison for criminally insane people to befriend suspected serial killer Larry Hall and find out where the bodies of several young girls are buried.

But Keene’s job isn’t easy because it’s not clear if Hall’s answers are true or just more lies from a habitual liar.

HOW MANY EPISODES ARE IN BLACK BIRD?

The whole season of Black Bird will have six episodes.

After the first two episodes came out on July 8, the rest of the series will come out every Friday.

WON’T THERE BE A SECOND SEASON?

No, there won’t be a second season of Black Bird on Apple TV+.

This is because the drama is called a “limited mini-series,” which means that its story is told in just one season.

Black Bird is a limited series because it is based on real events. The real James Keene wrote about these events in his 2010 autobiography, In With the Devil: A Fallen Hero, a Serial Killer, and a Dangerous Bargain for Redemption.

The book, like the show, is about Keene’s time in prison, but there is no sequel to the book, so there couldn’t be a second season.

Kris Jenner Told Us What Shes Shopping for Amazon Prime Big Deal Days

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, January 1, 0001

As an avid viewer of E’s Keeping Up With the Kardashians and now Hulu’s The Kardashians, I’m well-versed in Kris Jenner’s impeccable taste and epic gift-giving skills. Over the past few seasons, I’ve watched her build and decorate a home from the ground-up and surprise her kids with thoughtful, customized holiday gifts. So, when the opportunity arose to chat with Jenner about her must-have Prime Big Deal Days picks, I knew I had to find out what she’s buying from the October savings event. 

The momager and business mogul shared her favorite Prime Big Deal Days finds with InStyle over email, with prices starting at $19. (You can conveniently find them in her Amazon storefront, too.) Jenner said Amazon’s sale “gives Prime members exclusive early access to some of the best holiday savings,” adding that she “can easily shop holiday gifts for everyone on [her] list because of Amazon’s wide selection of products.” 

Kris Jenner’s Must-Have Deals

  • Donpapa Memory Foam Faux Fur Slippers, $20 (Originally $40)
  • Koolaburra by Ugg Advay Slip-on Fashion Boot, $65 (Originally $75)
  • Ekouaer Satin Pajamas Set, $19 (Originally $30)
  • Smirly Wood Charcuterie Board, $48 (Originally $60)
  • Aeezo 10.1-Inch WiFi Digital Picture Frame, $50 with coupon (Originally $70)
  • Keenray Towel Warmer, $107 with coupon (Originally $147)
  • Alanzimo Luxurious Goose Down Comforter, $116 (Originally $136)
  • Iniu Portable Charger, $29 (Originally $40)
  • Paris Hilton Iconic Nonstick 10-Piece Pots and Pans Set, $65 (Originally $130)
  • Breville Barista Express Impress Espresso Machine, $720 (Originally $900)


“For the holidays, I shop slippers,” Jenner said; more specifically, she added the Donpapa memory foam slippers to her list of favorite deals. On sale for half-off their original price — “a steal,” as Jenner put it — the slippers have a plush faux fur lining that extends onto the top for extra comfort and warmth. Plus, they have textured rubber outsoles, so you can even wear them outside to run quick errands. According to an Amazon shopper, the slippers feel like “walking on an incredibly warm, toasty cloud.” 

Another cozy footwear find, Jenner included the Koolaburra by Ugg Advay mini boots in her storefront. Discounted to $65, the suede booties have faux fur lining, anti-slip rubber outsoles, and a fabric pull-tab on the back for easy on-and-off. One shopper confirmed they “hit just at the ankle” and are the “perfect go-to shoe,” while a second person called them the “most comfortable shoes.” I don’t know about you, but I’m immediately clicking “Add to Cart.” 

Continuing on with the comfy vibes, Jenner chose this satin pajama set that’s on sale for just $19. The two-piece ensemble includes a V-neck T-shirt with lace trim and matching elastic-waist pants, which both come in eight solid colors. While you can certainly sleep in the set and wear it around the house with a pair of slippers, it’s stylish enough that you can get away with wearing it out and about, too. As one shopper said, the “quality, comfort, and fit are fantastic,” so you might as well get as much use out of the pieces as possible. 

From the home department, Jenner said this bamboo charcuterie board “would be perfect for holiday entertaining.” The package includes a multi-tier board with a set of cheese knives and serving forks, a wine opener, slate labels to identify the different snacks, and two ceramic bowls. Plus, a shopper confirmed the pieces “all come apart for easy cleaning or separate serving.” 

Jenner also “has [her] eyes” on the Breville Barista Express Impress espresso machine, she said, since “there is nothing better than a cup of coffee.” The high-tech gadget automatically doses out the amount of coffee you need for any given drink, so you don’t have to worry about mixing up the proportions. It also has a steam wand, so you can top off your beverages with delicious foam, just like in a coffee shop. A shopper said they “highly recommend this product to anyone looking for the perfect cup of coffee at home.” 

Of course, Jenner has a knack for interior design, so I was excited to find a chic comforter in her storefront. Available in five sizes, from a twin to a palatial king, the down-filled blanket has a box-stitch design to keep the filling evenly distributed throughout your bed. A reviewer even said the comforter “improved [their] sleep quality,” since it feels like “sleeping in a cloud.” And if you’re a hot sleeper, fear not — that same shopper said the blanket is “lightweight” while still keeping them “cozy at night.”

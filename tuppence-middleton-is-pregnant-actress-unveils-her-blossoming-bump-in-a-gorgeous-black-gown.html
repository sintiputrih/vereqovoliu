Tuppence Middleton is pregnant! Actress unveils her blossoming bump in a gorgeous black gown

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, May 24, 2024

Tuppence Middleton is pregnant with her first child. 

The actress, 35, unveiled her blossoming bump for the first time on Monday night as she attended the Downton Abbey: A New Era premiere at Leicester Square’s Cineworld. 

Beaming for the cameras on the red carpet, an expectant Tuppence proudly cupped her stomach as she wowed in a gorgeous black gown.

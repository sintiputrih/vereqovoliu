Michelle Harrison Husband Matthew Harrison Is An Actor Married To Her Since 1998

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, May 26, 2024

Michelle Harrison husband Matthew Harrison is an actor and acting coach. The couple has been together for over two decades.

Michelle is an actress known for playing the role of Nora Allen in the television series The Flash alongside Grant Gustin, Candice Patton, Andy Mientus, and Carlos Valdes. In 2009, she appeared in the show Wild Roses in a main role for 13 episodes. 

Her other acting credits include Out of Order, Continuum, Big Sky River, Hit’n Strum, and Fifty Shades Freed.

The actress’ spouse began his career in the entertainment industry as a writing assistant to an American playwright Frederick Douglas Wright. He then continued with his art education at the Neighbourhood Playhouse Theatre School.

Later, he was hired by the National Theatre of Performing Arts. The actor continued with stage performances for several years before appearing in movies and television shows. 

Matthew Harrison Is An Actor And Acting Coach

Matthew Harrison is known for appearing in the television series Chaos. Harrison is also the founder of The Actor’s Foundry.

Matthew has been in the entertainment industry for over two decades and has appeared in various films and television shows. He has appeared in a recurring role in the series Garage Sale Mysteries as Drew. 

In 2019, he portrayed the role of Mr. Wallis in Riverdale. Harrison has acted in the films such as Watchmen, Night at the Museum: Secret of the Tomb, and Seventeen and Missing.

He is also an acting coach who has been working in acting studios and schools since 1996. With the help of his coaching, he has been able to bring people’s acting passion into the profession. 

In 2007, he founded the company The Actor’s Foundry which provides training, mentorship, and workshops, to actors in order to succeed in the film, television, and theatre industry. 

The Actor’s Foundry has an official YouTube channel that includes videos of acting tips, analysis, and lessons.

Michelle And Matthew Have Been Married Since 1998

Michelle and Matthew Harrison tied the knot on June 6, 1998. The couple has been married for 24 years. 

The married couple first met each other in 1996, they dated for two years before they decided to take their relationship to next level. Despite being together for 26 years their love for each other hasn’t decreased.

The pair take some time out of their busy schedule to go on date nights or on a family vacation. The actress is seen sharing pictures of her husband on her social accounts on several occasions such as anniversaries and Valentine’s Day.

They have been private about their love story with the public.

The Couple Has An Age Difference Of Seven Years

Matthew Harrison is 54 years old and his wife is 47 years old.

Matthew celebrated his birthday on December 17 and his zodiac sign is Sagittarius. Michelle’s birth anniversary falls on March 24 and her zodiac sign is Aries.

The couple grew up in different parts of the world, the acting coach was born and raised in Montreal, Canada while the actress grew up in Puyallup, USA. 

The Flash star never misses a chance to wish her spouse on the occasion of his birthday, she shares pictures of them together along with heartwarming messages.

They Share Two Children Together

The couple has welcomed two sons into their life. The older son’s name is Ethan while the second child’s name hasn’t been disclosed by the pair.

Ethan came into this world in 2007 and he is 15 years old. He celebrates his birthday on April 12 and his zodiac sign is Aries. According to Michelle’s Instagram post, the couple welcomed their second son via adoption.

On September 29, 2022, the actress shared an image with her sons on the occasion of National Sons Day. During the occasion of Mother’s Day, The Flash star wrote a sweet message to her kids mentioning that she is endlessly thankful that they were part of her life. 

Michelle Grew Up In A Family Of Five

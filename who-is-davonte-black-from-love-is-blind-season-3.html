Who Is DaVont Black From Love Is Blind Season 3?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, January 13, 2024

On Wednesday, October 19, 30 new singles will join the popular Netflix program Love is Blind to discover their ideal partners. While the candidates will be unable to see each other, they will be able to converse in a pod separated by a wall. They will be allowed ten days to fall in love and get engaged before they can finally meet. The couples will then go on their honeymoon and meet their partners’ families before choosing whether or not to say “I do” at the wedding altar.

29-year-old DaVonté Black is one of the singles seeking love this season. He’s a fitness fanatic looking for someone “bold, powerful, independent, fitness-loving, and smart.”

Who Is DaVonté Black?

DaVonté is a native of Rowlett, Texas. He attended Lakeview Centennial High School, where he earned three football letters. He also ran track in school and received two letters. He did, however, retire from athletics at the age of 24. DaVonté has spent the last five years as a fitness and development coach. A typical meeting with him is included in his $250 four-week DIY training program.

He specializes in athletic performance, functional training, and muscle development, and he has worked with both beginners and those with over 30 years of fitness expertise. DaVonté works at SMTF Dallas and has a fitness Instagram account with the handle @db purestrength. He says he works out often because he takes fitness seriously. He suspects that his fitness obsession is getting on his partner’s nerves. DaVonté’s personal Instagram account, @black sparrow23, now has around 1500 followers.

What can we anticipate from Season 3 of Love is Blind?

The teaser for Love is Blind Season 3 teases some high-octane drama, with one guy suggesting that they “change” fiancées. Another cast member wonders whether he’ll meet his wife in the grocery store, while another is hoping to meet her spouse on the program. Some cast members are even observed telling “crazy” tales to their unidentified spouses.

The honeymoon period is also predicted to be crazy, since the team has been spotted partying, and one guy has said that he has many unanswered concerns about his fiancée. Another cast member will be shown on the honeymoon feeling drawn to various ladies. The teaser also teases other weddings, with one woman shown walking away from the altar.

The next Dallas season began filming immediately after the conclusion of Love is Blind Season 2. The show’s creator, Chris Coelen, told Metro that the upcoming season will be substantially different from Seasons 1 and 2. Among the couples that have previously married in front of the cameras are:

  • Matt Barnett and Amber Pike
  • Nick Thompson and Danielle Ruhl (Now Divorced)
  • Jarrette Jones and Iyanna McNeely (Now Divorced)
  • Cameron Hamilton and Lauren Speed

The first four episodes of Season 3 of Love is Blind will be available on Netflix on October 19 at 3 p.m. ET. Episodes 5–7 will be released on October 26, followed by the following three episodes on Netflix on November 2.

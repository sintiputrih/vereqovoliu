Jonathan Bennett Set To Star In Gay Rom-Com 'The Christmas House'

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, March 14, 2024

Hallmark is setting up things for the Mean Girls star Jonathan Bennett to star in the gay Christmas rom-com act with The Christmas House. The movie will also costar Robert Buckley from One Tree Hill, Ana Ayora from In The Dark, and Treat Williams, Sharon Lawrence, and Brad Harder from Good Morning America.

The Christmas House will surround the story of a gay couple who tries to adopt a child in their family. In the quest, they are heading to see their family, the executive vice president of programming for Crown Media Family Network, Michelle Vicary mentioned. He further mentioned:

'Our holiday table is bigger and more welcoming than ever. This year's movies reflect our most diverse representation of talent, narratives, and families. Our movies are rooted in warmth and positivity, meaningful connections, family gatherings, and seasonal traditions — a winning formula we hope will bring our millions of viewers much-needed levity and holiday cheer at the end of a tough year."

The original synopsis of the movie reads:

"Working through some difficult decisions, Mitchell family matriarch Phylis (Lawrence) and patriarch Bill (Williams), have summoned their two grown sons — Mike (Buckley) and Brandon (Bennett) — home for the holidays. It is their hope that bringing the family together to recreate the Christmas house will help them find resolution and make a memorable holiday for the entire family and community. As Brandon and his husband, Jake (Harder), make the trip home, they are anxiously awaiting a call about the adoption of their first child. Meanwhile, Mike reconnects with Andi (Ayora), his high school sweetheart"

Hallmark movie is the latest line of the film, which features the movie with LGBTQ stories. It includes projects like Let It Snow, The Thing About Harry, The Christmas Setup, and many others.

Jonathan Bennett might be the latest actor likely to get started in the Hallway movie, The Christmas House.

Jonathan Bennett And His Partner

Jonathan Bennett is currently in a same-sex relationship with his partner, Jaymes Vaughan. They have been cherishing their love life for a few years now and often consider themselves ready for the next step in the relationship.

Talking about their love life, Jonathan Bennett mentioned that he would like to engage with his partner soon in a flash mob. 'I want a flash mob, just saying, with drag queens,' he mentioned.

Jonathan Bennett and Jaymes Vaughan (©: Instagram/Jaymes Vaughan)

Bennett, along with his love interest, Jaymes Vaughan, opened about their plans to the Hollywood Life during the premiere of Netflix's new show AJ & The Queen at Hollywood's Egyptian Theatre on 9 January 2020. Bennett added:

"I’m a control freak…if we’re going to get married, I want to propose because I want to control it and make it perfect and amazing and then I realized I would have to do all the work if I… If he’s the one that does it, all I have to do is show up and say, yes.”

It has been three years since Bennett and Vaughan started dating. After they initiated their love life in 2017, they made it public on Instagram on the Halloween of the same year. 

Jonathan Bennet Net Worth

Jonathan Bennett garners the net worth of $1 million from his professional career as an American actor.

He landed his first role in the 1997 television series named Dad. The following year, he appeared on another tv series named The Bill in 1998. Since then, he starred in several movies and tv shows, including All My Children (2001-02), Season of Youth (2003), Cane (2007), The Mean Bean (2010), and Memorial Day (2012).

Likewise, Bennett has appeared in The Glades (2013), Math Bites (2014), Cats Dancing on Jupiter (2015), Awkward. (2016), Surprise Me! (2017), Supergirl (2019), Station 19 (2020), and many others.

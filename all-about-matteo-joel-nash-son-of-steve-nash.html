All About Matteo Joel Nash, Son Of Steve Nash

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, August 25, 2024

As a celebrity child, Matteo Joel Nash is well-known. He is the son of Steve Nash, a former player, and Canadian professional basketball coach. During his 18-year playing career, he was an eight-time All-Star, a seven-time All-NBA pick, and a two-time MVP winner. He will take over as head coach of the Brooklyn Nets on September 3rd, 2020.

Net Worth of Matteo Joel Nash

Matteo has yet to begin his career and is currently living in luxury with his parents. Steve has amassed a substantial net income as a result of his playing career.

He was earning $10 million per year by the end of his career in 2015. As of January 2023, Matteo Joel Nash’s net worth was $95 million. Head coach salary of $8.7 million, brand endorsements, business ventures, and author and producer of countless Nike commercials

Date of Birth and Age

In the year 2010, Matteo was born. He is currently ten years old. He celebrates his birthday on November 12th.

Steve Nash’s first failed marriage

Steve married film director Alejandra Amarilla in June 2005. In 2001, the couple first met in Manhattan. After dating for three years, they had their twin daughters, Lola and Bella, on October 14, 2004. Their marriage, however, did not last long, and they divorced after nine years.

His parents’ divorce, Alejandra and Steve, went public on the same day he was born when Steve announced that they were divorcing. In addition to the happy news of Matteo’s birth, he announced the sad news of his divorce from his first wife.

According to him, they had been living apart for some months. With the announcement came suspicions about Alejandra’s relationship with Steve’s coworker Jason Richardson, leading some to suspect Matteo was not his child.

The rumor, however, was later proven to be false. Alejandra stated that the couple desired a son after having two daughters and had been planning Matteo for a year.

Matteo has two older sisters, Lola and Bella

He is the younger brother of his six-year-old twin sisters, Lola Nash and Bella Nash. Matteo gets a lot of attention from his twin sisters because he is the youngest. He also has two paternal half-siblings named Luca and Ruby.

Conflict over custody and its settlement

The ex-couple fought for custody of their three children after their divorce. The court, however, determined that Steve was not required to compensate Alejandra because she was earning more than him at the time. When Steve was dealt to the Los Angeles Lakers in 2012, she did not pass up the opportunity for the second time.

She sought child support in California courts, but Steve was opposed to her plan to relocate from Arizona to Los Angeles and refused to assist her.

He did not give his ex-wife any money, but he did pay for 90% of the children’s medical, educational, and extracurricular activities fees, as well as 80% of the nannies’ income. They eventually worked out their differences.

The Second Wedding

Steve is currently married to Lilla Frederick, a former volleyball player. Steve proposed to Lilla in 2016, after she said yes on a romantic vacation in Majorca. They married in 2017.

It was an outdoor wedding ceremony at Manhattan Beach, California, attended by close friends, family, and NBA All-Stars. The couple welcomed their first kid, Luca, in 2016 and their second child, Ruby, in July 2019. They are currently residing in Brooklyn.

The Origin Story

Kayleigh Swenson was born on October 22, 1985, in San Antonio, Texas, and is the daughter of the late Robert Swenson, a professional wrestler, and actor.

Her father’s most notable role was Bane in the 1997 Batman and Robin.

Her father died on August 18, 1997, when she was only twelve years old.

Kayleigh was the only child of her parents.

She was raised by her mother, Erin Swenson. Her mother was a successful model and dancer.

Jail and Rehab

Swenson expressed how 2011 had been the toughest time of her life.

She had just gotten out of jail and was struggling with drug addiction.

Before she was admitted to rehab, she was struggling physically.

Kayleigh claimed that her hair had been falling off, her skin was ruined, and she had lost some teeth.

She feared that she would not have survived, but she did and is better than before.

“I was using drugs for like 10 years,” Swenson said while being invited to the Dumb Blonde podcast with Bunnie Xo, the wife of Jelly Roll. “I was 16 or 16 on, got sober … went to a low-bottom rehab. It’s humbling. I didn’t go to the places that have massages and the Olympic pools. I went to a free basically rehab.”

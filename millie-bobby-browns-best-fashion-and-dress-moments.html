Millie Bobby Browns Best Fashion and Dress Moments

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, June 3, 2024

There’s something about Millie Bobby Brown. The actress has enjoyed a rapid ascent to style icon status on and off the red carpet.

Some of her greatest red carpet moments were brought to life by her stylist, Thomas Carter Phillips. The style guru has cultivated a whimsical and age-appropriate wardrobe for Brown that includes flirty frocks, darling dresses, chic separates and more. 

For the 2018 Screen Actors Guild Awards, Brown looked pretty in a pink sequined Calvin Klein mullet dress, which she paired with white Converse sneakers. Her bow-adorned space buns and rosy lips finished the playful look. 

After stepping out in a number of other looks by Calvin Klein, the Enola Holmes actress moved on to another major fashion house. In April 2022, Louis Vuitton named Brown as a brand ambassador. “I met [creative director] Nicolas Ghesquière six years ago and have been a fan of his work with Louis Vuitton ever since,” she said in a statement at the time, per Women’s Wear Daily. “Today, I am proud and honored to be joining the Louis Vuitton family. Truly a pinch me, full circle moment.” 

The following month, Brown celebrated her relationship with the luxury label at the Stranger Things season 4 premiere in New York City. On the red carpet, she turned up the heat in a white silk gown by Louis Vuitton that featured a high slit and a belt at the bodice. 

For glam, Brown rocked a subtle but noticeable metallic eye, rosy cheeks and a glossy lip — courtesy of makeup artist Buster Knight. The glowing beat featured products from Brown’s Florence by Mills cosmetics label. She launched the beauty venture in August 2019, calling the brand the “love” of her life. The idea and direction came straight from the young beauty’s personal experience. “I wanted to come into the space because there was a gap in the market for young people,” Brown told WWD at the time. “I guess I could never find anything that I liked to put on my face and it felt good. I’d take off my makeup and boom, another pimple would appear.”

What is Michael Redds Net Worth: Biography,Net Worth &amp; more

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, July 13, 2024

Michael Redd is an American former professional basketball player who has a net worth of US$30 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth. He starred for the Milwaukee Bucks and Phoenix Suns over the course of a 12 season career in the National Basketball Association (NBA). The Ohio-born basketball player was drafted 43rd overall in the 2000 NBA draft by the Milwaukee Bucks where he later 11 of his 12 seasons as a professional player.

Who is Michael Redd?

Michael Wesley Redd was born August 24, 1979 in Columbus, Ohio in the United States. He is the son of Hajj Redd and James Redd. He has a sister named Mitchelle. Michael is an American national and of African descent.

Redd attended West High School in Columbus, Ohio. After graduation, Redd enrolled to the Ohio State University, where he spent three years playing college basketball as a shooting guard/small forward.

During his collegiate years, Redd averaged 21.9, 19.5 and 17.5 points respectively. As a sophomore, he and Scoonie Penn led the Ohio State University to the NCAA Final Four. After his junior year, he was drafted in the 2000 NBA draft as a second round pick (43rd overall) by the Milwaukee Bucks.

Redd starred for the Bucks for a total of 11 seasons. In his rookie year, he was not able to hit the ground running, falling behind NBA All-Star Ray Allen on the depth chart. He stepped up his game and proved to be a better than Ray Allen, Glenn Robinson and other teammates.

Over the course of his career, Redd was awarded NBA All-Star (2004), All-NBA Third Team (2004) and Big Ten Freshman of the Year (1998). He won gold in the 1999 Palma, 2007 Las Vegas and 2008 Beijing at the Olympic games.

Lisa Barlow Sings You a Christmas Carol

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, February 9, 2024

Day 25: Let It Barlow

Twenty-five moments, one per day in the walkup to Christmas, that brought definition to reality TV’s ever-blurring lines in 2023.

Lisa Barlow sets the bar high (hehehe) for Real Housewives across this nation. Never mind the convicted financial criminals and possible cult leaders; it was Barlow’s sheer charisma, her lovable personality and way with words, that ensured the Real Housewives of Salt Lake City would not just be a flyover franchise. Among her myriad gifts, Barlow revealed herself to be something of a songstress and Christmas queen in season three, singing a beautiful rendition of “Away in a Manger” that has some fans clamoring for the full holiday EP. As a special Christmas present to the baby gorgeouses of Vulture’s readership, she has recorded another holiday classic, just for you.

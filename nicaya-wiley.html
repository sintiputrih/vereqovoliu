Nicaya Wiley is an American dancer notable for her appearances on the reality television program ‘Dance Moms’. Nicaya Wiley is also a choreographer, singer, model, actress & anti-bullying advocate.

Early Life and Childhood

Nicaya Wiley came to this world on May 17, 2001, in Kirkwood, MO, USA. She is 22 years old as of 2024 and her birth sign is Taurus. Her mother’s name is Kaya Wiley. She has a sister named A’maya and brothers named Michael, Nico, and Dennis. Moreover, she graduated from high school on May 27, 2019.

Professional Life

Nicaya made her debut on the show in the August 2012 episode “New Girl in Town.” She later appeared in the third season episodes “Bye Bye Baby” and “An Apple a Day Keeps Abby Away”. And, before performing with the Abby Lee Dance Company, she trained at both the Center of Creative Arts and the Charmette Academy of Dance in St. Louis, Missouri. Moreover, she and Madison Ziegler were both featured in the season four Dance Moms episode “Two Can Play This Game.”

She was first introduced to Dance Moms during the second season after Abby decided to try her out on the elite team. However, Nicaya and her mother did not return the following week.  She is a singer as well as a dancer, she shows off her vocal skills in several YouTube videos uploaded by her mother.

Nicaya later returned the following season in Bye Bye Baby, where she completed a solo against Nia and Maddie. She later went on to be part of the CADC team for the third and part of the fourth season. She and her mother did not return for the following two seasons, but they have recently re-joined the Candy Apples for Season 7.

Nicaya also has her self-titled YouTube channel which was joined on March 2, 2014. Some of her most popular YouTube videos are ‘Kaya addressing the Dance Moms drama’, ‘Nicaya FINALLY learns acro tricks!’, ‘Nicaya Wiley-Heartburn’, ‘Nicaya’s British Accent’, ‘Nicaya Wiley & Camryn Bridges’, ‘Nicaya Wiley-Bust Your Windows’, ‘Nicaya’s Turns’, and many more.

Who Is America Lopez? Her Boyfriend, Parents, Job

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, June 12, 2024

America Lopez, a medical receptionist, is a mysterious contestant on Big Brother Season 25. Her name alone has generated some conversation on Reddit, the one who started the thread believed that she is a fake identity.

The cast for this year’s season will be diverse in terms of their ages and backgrounds, as usual. Contestants give up their careers and live with 15 strangers in order to participate in the show. Over 100 microphones and over 100 cameras are constantly recording everything they do. They compete along the way for prizes and the opportunity to either nominate house guests for eviction or to defend themselves when nominated.

A house guest gets evicted once every week. House guests must develop covert connections in order to live, which invariably results in drama and backstabbing.

Find out more about America here in this bio.

America Lopez On Big Brother 25

Edinburg native America Lopez will join 15 other contestants in the Big Brother house for a chance to win $750,000. The CBS reality TV show, in its 25th season, is in August 2023.

America has been waiting for season 25 and it is a huge deal for her. She added, “This is a big season to be on. I’m so excited and so ready to play. I can’t believe that I get to be part of this historic moment, I’m ready to play.” Being a superfan of the show, she has been watching the show for the last 15 years.

America explained to Parade, “So I’m going to be in here lying, backstabbing, and blindsiding with whoever I can, whenever I get the chance. I plan to stay low at the beginning, just because I feel like that strategy has worked for past players. But I’m definitely just going to be the sweet, bubbly, charismatic person and try to get to know everybody and be genuine with them.”

The Texas resident has a couple of strategies by laying low at the beginning and then she is going to play the kitchen strategy and just try to get on all the houseguests’ good side when it comes to that.

America, a recent graduate of Edinburg High, received congratulations from the Edinburg Consolidated Independent School District. “America Lopez, a proud Edinburg High School alum, is all set to grace our screens in Season 25 of Big Brother on CBS! Congratulations America on this amazing opportunity,” the district said on Facebook. 

America is confident that she will win. She added, “I’m the one to watch out for in this game because I know this game. I’ve watched it for 24 seasons. I’m gonna go in here full throttle, just playing 1,000% all the time.”

America Lopez Job

America Lopez has a job as a Medical Receptionist. Per a Facebook post, America attended Brown University. She was also a member of BU’s cheerleading team. Besides that, there isn’t much about her job and career.

Fun Fact: America has a PoshMark profile (@americalopez200).

How Old Is America Lopez?

America Lopez was 27 years old when she debuted on Big Brother in 2023.

America Lopez Boyfriend

Currently, there are no details about America Lopez’s love life and current relationship status. She is very private as a matter of fact and doesn’t seem to be on social media.

America Lopez Height

The beautiful America Lopez’s height measures under 5 feet 5 inches.

Who Are America Lopez Parents?

While appearing on the show, America Lopez shared briefly about her family members. She told Parade, “I’m just worried about being homesick. I am a huge family girl. I talk to my mom every day. We have a big group chat with me and all my five siblings.”

She added further, “So we’re just constantly talking back and forth. So I’m worried about missing them while I’m in here. But other than that, I’m ready to play, and I’m ready to have a fun summer.”

Besides that, there isn’t much about America’s family. The public record reveals that her parents are likely named Lonerna Lopez and Eduardo Lopez.

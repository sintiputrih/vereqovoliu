Who Is Allure Mitchell? Meet Kel Mitchell Daughter! Her Job, Age

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, May 29, 2024

Allure Mitchell, daughter of actor Kel Mitchell, decided to take to TikTok to bash her father. Over the years the father-daughter duo had a tumultuous relationship. How old is Allure? What does she do? Which college is she attending now? Does she have a boyfriend?

This article answers all the questions.

Meet Allure Mitchell, Kel Mitchell Daughter

Kel Mitchell was previously married to Tyisha Hampton with whom he had a daughter named Allure. In October 2018, Kel wished Allure a birthday via an IG post, “Everyone wish my beautiful and smart daughter a very special Happy Birthday! Wow!! Seventeen years old today! Love you Allure!! ❤️ #proudFather #daddydaughtertime #HappyBirthday.”

But, the relationship between the two appears to have soured over the years. It all started when Allure’s mother claimed that Kel hasn’t paid her financial support. In accordance with court records, Tyisha allegedly sent the actor’s lawyer an email requesting an urgent court hearing.

In the wake of this legal battle, Allure decided to blast her father on TikTok. Allure Mitchell claimed that she and her father haven’t had a proper relationship for ten years. She claimed that she has neither provided financial nor any emotional support to her and her family over the years.

Allure said in her TikTok: “Me, myself, I was homeless, my family is still homeless, my mom still doesn’t have a house because he sold the house from my mother that we were all living in. And I was young and my mom spent years and years and years fighting for it. That’s what I’m mad at him for.”

Additionally, Allure disclosed that she frequently traveled two to three hours to see her father in an effort to establish a relationship with him. She also made a TikTok video, in which she included a recording of herself and her father. She wrote a post: “I’m not a normal dad I don’t work a 9-5.”

Allure claimed that it was “narcissistic” of her father to claim that his career obstructed him from having a parenting role. She then added that Kel only supported him financially when it looked good on paper for him like the time when her high school tuition, housing on campus, and her first car.

In May 2019, when Allure graduated from high school, Kel proudly posted on his IG, “She did it!! 👩🏽‍🎓 Congratulations  @alluremariah So proud of all your accomplishments and you have a blessed, bright beautiful future ahead! I love you so much! And I am very proud of you! #Godisgood #classof2019 SWIPE LEFT ⬅️ to see highlights from the celebration.”

Allure Mitchell Mom And Siblings

As said already, Allure Mitchell was born from the marriage between Kel and her mother Tyisha Hampton. Tyisha reportedly met Kel at a Hollywood party in 1996. After dating for three years, they exchanged their wedding vows on 23 April 1999. However, they called it quits on 6 August 2005.

According to RadarOnline, Tyisha has accused him of owing her $1.2 million which Kel has denied. They appeared before the judge on August 17. The two are currently at odds over a Pasadena house. Kel claims credit in the case when Tyisha was given the house as part of the divorce settlement.

Tyisha asked the court to look into allegations of criminal behavior made during the most recent session by counsel and a court official. The appeal was turned down. The specifics of Tyisha’s accusations were kept a secret.

Talking about Allure’s mom, Tyisha was born on 17 June 1978, making her 44 years old in 2022. As an actress, she has appeared on Maya & Miguel, Kenan & Kel, Voyeur: In Hindsight, Cousin Skeeter, and a few other shows.

About her siblings, she has an older brother named Lyric Mitchell. Kel turned 23 years old in April 2022. On his 18th birthday in 2017, Kel posted a clip of his birthday celebration and posted, “My Son Lyric turned 18! Love you son!! You’re turning out to be such a great young man! God bless!!”

Additionally, Allure also has two half-siblings; a sister named Wisdom, 5, and a brother named Honor, 2, whom her dad shares with his second wife, Asia Lee.

Allure Mitchell Age

Allure Mitchell is 21 years old. She was born on 31 October 2001.

Is Allure Mitchell On TikTok And Instagram?

Yes, Allure Mitchell is on Instagram (@alluremariah).

Allure Mitchell Job

Allure Mitchell graduated from St. Lucy’s High School in 2019. Since July 2019, she has enrolled at Cal Poly Pomona where she is studying International Business & Marketing where her LinkedIn reveals that she is studying BBA with a major in Computer and Information Systems and is expected to graduate in 2023.

Talking about her job, Allure has worked as a student intern at Assemblymember Chris Holden’s 41st district office in Pasadena, CA. She also served as a clerical assistant at Pasadena City Hall for three months in 2019. Allure is currently working as a student assistant at California State Polytechnic University-Pomona.

Is Allure Mitchell Dating Anyone?

On her Facebook, Allure Mitchell revealed in August 2020 that she is in a relationship. But, she chose not to share anything about her boyfriend. There are no pictures of her partner on her Facebook and her IG is currently private so there isn’t a way to dig up more information related to her relationship.

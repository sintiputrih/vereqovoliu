Maluma family, wife, kids, parents, siblings

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, January 27, 2024

Juan Luis Londoo Arias (born 28 January 1994) is a Colombian singer, songwriter, and actor better known as Maluma.

He was born and raised in Medelln, where he had an early interest in music and began recording songs at the age of sixteen.

His breakthrough album, Pretty Boy, Dirty Boy, was released in 2015, and it paved the path for successful collaborations with a diverse spectrum of artists. In 2018, he released F.A.M.E., another commercial triumph. He followed it up with 11:11 in 2019 and Papi Juancho, which dropped unexpectedly in 2020.

His single “Hawái” peaked at number three on the Billboard Global 200 and was the first to top the Billboard Global Exclusivity Index (BGEI).
Maluma is one of the most successful Latin music artists, having sold over 18 million records worldwide.

What is Patrick Mahomes' Rookie Card Worth in 2024?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, May 26, 2024

Patrick Mahomes, the star quarterback for the Kansas City Chiefs, has not only achieved remarkable success on the football field but has also become a highly sought-after figure in the world of sports collectibles, particularly when it comes to his rookie cards. 

Factors Affecting the Value of Patrick Mahomes' Rookie Card

1. Condition

The condition of a card is one of the most crucial factors influencing its value. Cards in pristine condition, without any creases, stains, or other flaws, are highly sought after by collectors, commanding a premium price.

2. Scarcity

The scarcity of a card plays a significant role in determining its value. Limited edition or low print run cards, such as rookie cards, are typically more valuable due to their rarity.

3. Autograph or Memorabilia

Many rookie cards feature autographs or pieces of game-worn memorabilia, such as jersey patches or footballs, adding an extra layer of uniqueness and collectability to the card, thereby increasing its value.

4. Popularity and Performance

The popularity of the player and their on-field performance can greatly influence the value of their rookie card. Patrick Mahomes' exceptional success and growing fan base have undoubtedly contributed to the high demand for his cards.

Most Valuable Patrick Mahomes Rookie Cards

1. 2017 Panini National Treasures Patrick Mahomes RC Auto Patch #161/99

This rookie card is a cornerstone card from Panini, featuring only 99 copies in the world. The value added to the card is due to the on-card signature and a large patch on the card. The NFL Shield version of this card is currently the most expensive NFL rookie card.

2. 2017 Panini Flawless Patrick Mahomes RC Autograph Patch/Helmet #107/75

This special edition rookie card from Panini is one of the rarest cards, even rarer than the most valuable National Treasures card. It combines patches, Mahomes' autograph, and a helmet piece along with a superb design.

3. 2017 Panini Flawless Patrick Mahomes RC Autograph #132/20

This retro-styled rookie card is one of the rarest with only 20 copies. This signed rookie card has a gemstone attached to it, making it highly valuable.

4. 2017 Panini Contenders Patrick Mahomes RC Autograph #303

This valuable Mahomes rookie card, limited to about 250 copies, is a collector's favorite.

5. 2017 Panini Contenders Optic Patrick Mahomes RC Autograph #103

With a print limited to around only 300 copies, this card's Optichrome technology and on-card autograph of Patrick Mahomes make it highly sought after.

Estimated Value in 2024

The estimated value of Patrick Mahomes' rookie cards in 2024 varies greatly depending on factors such as condition, scarcity, and the presence of autographs or memorabilia.

On average, a base rookie card from reputable brands like Panini or Topps can range from a few hundred dollars to several thousand dollars.

Cards in pristine condition, with autographs or memorabilia, can command even higher prices, often reaching five or six figures.

One of the most notable sales is the 2017 National Treasures NFL Shield Mahomes Rookie Auto 1/1 BGS 8.5 card, which sold for a staggering $4.3 million, making it the most expensive football rookie card ever sold.

This card's value is expected to rise even further over the years, given its rarity and the increasing demand for Mahomes' memorabilia.

Recent Sales

In August 2023, a PSA 10 graded 2017 Panini National Treasures Patrick Mahomes Rookie Patch Auto card was sold through Goldin Auctions for $193,000.

This card, bearing an unblemished autograph of Patrick Mahomes, has seen a meteoric rise in value over the years.

Another significant sale occurred in August 2023, where a PSA 10 graded 2017 Panini Flawless Patrick Mahomes Rookie Patch Auto card was sold through PWCC Auctions for $66,000.

This card features an authentic on-card autograph, a three-color patch, and is limited to just 25 copies, making it highly desirable among collectors.

Investment Insight

Navigating through Patrick Mahomes' rookie cards, the National Treasures Rookie Patch Auto Card emerges as both a visual masterpiece and a robust financial beacon.

The card has shown a significant rise in value from 2018 to 2021, reaching a high of $399,750 in a Goldin Auction. Although the value dipped to $193,066 in September 2023, it is still up from its 2020 value, underlining its colossal investment trajectory.

Rarity and Design

The National Treasures Rookie Patch Auto card seamlessly melds artistic brilliance with exclusivity.

It exhibits a radiant autograph below a portrayal of Mahomes in his NFL ensemble, featuring prominently tri-colored patches that intricately highlight segments from Mahomes' jersey, adding to its rarity and authenticity.

Also, the card series flaunts exclusive editions, including 1 of 1 versions and Printing Plates, each exuding unique color gradations.

 

On the whole, the estimated value of Patrick Mahomes' rookie card in 2024 continues to be influenced by factors such as condition, scarcity, the presence of autographs or memorabilia, and the player's popularity and performance.

With recent sales of his rookie cards reaching hundreds of thousands of dollars, it is evident that Patrick Mahomes' rookie cards remain a valuable and highly sought-after collectible in the sports card market.

With the continued popularity of football trading cards and Mahomes' exceptional skills, his rookie cards are likely to remain valuable and highly sought-after collectibles for years to come.

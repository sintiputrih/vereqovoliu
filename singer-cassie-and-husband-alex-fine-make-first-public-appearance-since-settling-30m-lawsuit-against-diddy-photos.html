Singer Cassie and Husband Alex Fine Make First Public Appearance Since Settling $30M Lawsuit Against

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, April 7, 2024


Cassie was seen for the first time with her husband since filing a lawsuit against Sean ‘Diddy’ Combs.

The 37-year-old singer, accompanied by her partner, Alex Fine, and their two children, enjoyed a family outing and celebrated their eldest daughter’s early birthday meal at Serendipity 3 in New York City on Saturday, November 2.

The couple celebrated their daughter Frankie’s fourth birthday ahead of the actual date on December 6.

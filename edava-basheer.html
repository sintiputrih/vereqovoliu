Edava Basheer was a popular playback singer, musician, and media personality from Kollam, Kerala, India. He was well-known throughout the country for his incredible voice and beautiful singing. He primarily sang Malayalam songs.

He also founded the ‘Kollam Sangeethalaya Ganamela’ music troupe to teach the children. In addition, he has sung several Hindi songs and collaborated with numerous other singers. His songs’ titles included Marubhoomiyamee, Rahamathukal Niranjoru, Manjaninjeemanal, and many others.

Career

Edava Basheer has been in the spotlight since his school days due to his adorable voice and singing abilities. He won several singing awards in college and high school competitions. Later, he decided to focus solely on his singing career. After finishing his music studies, he entered the Malayalam music industry and later the Hindi music industry.

As a playback singer, he sang many songs. In the film Raghu Vamsham, he makes his singing debut as a playback singer. According to reports, he sang a song in praise of the goddess Durga called ‘Akasaroopini, Annapoorneswari.’ In addition, in 1972, he founded the ‘Kollam Sangeethalaya Ganamela’ music troupe in Thiruvananthapuram.

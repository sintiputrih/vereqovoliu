Gabriel Damon Career

Damon’s career began with appearances in numerous commercials, with his first role in the 1984 TV series Call to Glory. He voiced Littlefoot the Apatosaurus in the first The Land Before Time movie, and Little Nemo in Little Nemo: Adventures in Slumberland. In 1990 he appeared as the juvenile criminal Hob in RoboCop 2 and in 1992 as Spot Conlon in Newsies. Damon has also made guest appearances in television series, such as ER, Call to Glory, Star Trek: The Next Generation, and Baywatch. Damon retired from the entertainment industry in 2006, to work as a real estate agent.

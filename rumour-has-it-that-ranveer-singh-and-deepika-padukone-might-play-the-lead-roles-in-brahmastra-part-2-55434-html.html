Rumour has it that Ranveer Singh and Deepika Padukone might play the lead roles in Brahmastra Part 2

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, April 18, 2024

Ever since its inception - Brahmastra has wreaked havoc in the world of cinema. The larger-than-life visual spectacle had created a huge hype including the massive fan-following that the lead pair - Ranbir Kapoor and Alia Bhatt have. Now that the film has finally hit the theatres - the box office numbers are increasing by the day and it comes as no surprise.



This three-part trilogy already has a lot going for the first part that was released, now according to some humours - the second installment is most likely to have Deepika Padukone and Ranveer Singh in the lead. While some are confident that the original cast is most likely to be retained, a report in a news portal speculates that Ranveer Singh and Deepika Padukone would be signed to play the leads in part two.

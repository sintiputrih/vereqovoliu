Judah Smith Biography

Judah Smith is an American Pastor known for having led The City Church, which is based in Seattle, Washington. Alongside his wife Chelsea Smith, they lead pastors of the City Church in Seattle, Washington. He was born on October 9, 1978, in Washington, United States to his parents who were pastors. Judah’s father, Wendell, began the church in 1992. His mother helped his father co-run the church until Wendell’s passing in 2010.

Judah began his time as Lead Pastor of the church in 2009. Judah is also a speaker and travels the country to present at conferences and churches around the world.

Judah Smith Parents

Judah was born and raised by his parents Wendell Smith (Pastor) and Virginia “Gini” Melton (Pastor) in Washington. Judah has one sister named Wendy Perez. In 1992, his father Wendell started The City Church, a nondenominational, evangelical church in Seattle. His congregation started off small with twenty-one people and by 2010 it grew to over 6,000 people on six campuses. Judah first got the urge to preach when he was nine and had a supernatural encounter with God when he was sixteen. In high school, Judah was a star basketball player.

Judah Smith Wife

Judah is married to his beloved wife Chelsea Smith and they have three children together, two sons named Zion Smith and Eliott Smith and one daughter named Grace Smith. Judah is an avid golfer as well as an all-around sports fan. Judah believes the Seahawks are God’s favorite team and is praying for the Sonics to come back to Seattle.

Who Is James Droz Of The What Happened NFL Meme As He Is Banned From TikTok For Football Commentary

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, August 2, 2024

James Droz is a sports fan who seems to have been banned from his main TikTok account for his videos on football

At the time of this writing, his @jamesdroz566 profile had just shy of 900K followers. But the ban hasn’t stopped Droz’s face from getting around the Internet like wildfire.

Many accounts with very similar TikTok handles, like @jamesdroz568, @.jamesdroz566, and so on, have popped up around it.

Know Who Is James Droz Of The What Happened NFL Meme As He Is Banned From TikTok For Football Commentary

Fans of James Droz have been complaining about the ban on TikTok and other social media sites (like Twitter) and talking about what might have caused it. So, what went wrong?

James Droz is from Bartlesville, and ever since he was a little boy, he’s loved sports. Susan Hainzinger, Droz’s mother, says that he used to remember the results of every football game he saw. James played many sports when he was younger. His TikTok account showed how much he loved sports, and his fans are sad that his account has been banned.

James Droz, whose TikTok username is @jamesdrozz566, was reportedly banned for breaking company rules. Fans are pleading with TikTok to let him back on because they like what he posts. TikTok has not yet said anything officially about the ban.

Droz played football for the Bartlesville team, but because of his special needs, he wasn’t always able to play on the field. He did help the coach of their team, though. Even when he scored a touchdown against Choctaw the year before, Examiner-Enterprise said, the fans cheered for James the most.

James Droz “What happened?” NFL Meme

Jamesdroz566’s TikTok presence began on October 31, 2021, when he posted a duet of TikToker wehoopin’s video showing that the Cincinnati Bengals had lost to the New York Jets. In a year, it got about 39,900 plays and 1,000 likes.

The video by TikToker wehoopin was posted earlier in the day and has gotten about 695,200 views and 59,300 likes on its own. In the video, TikToker wehoopin yelled, “Bengals, what happened?” This is where Jamesdroz566 got the idea to use the phrase later on.

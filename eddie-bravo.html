Biography

Eddie Bravo was born in Santa Ana, CA on May 15, 1970. Brazilian jiu-jitsu practitioner and instructor who is best known for his choke-out of Royler Gracie while still a brown belt. This grappling win took place at the 2003 Abu Dhabi Submission Wrestling Championships. He has trained various well-known fighters, including Ronda Rousey and Chuck Liddell. He began wrestling in high school, which later led to his burgeoning interest in Brazilian jiu-jitsu. In April 1996, he first enrolled in jiu-jitsu classes in Hollywood, California.
